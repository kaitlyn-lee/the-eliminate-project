Be the Hero

How You Can Help

With your fundraising and monetary donations, UNICEF, along with Kiwanis International will provide vaccinations and other life-saving measures for women in developing countries. This partnership is known as the Eliminate Project. So far, nearly 100 million women have already been vaccinated against the disease, but some countries still have yet to eliminate MNT. Learn how everyone can save our children of the world using the checklist below!

Want to know how people of all ages and occupations can help AND have fun? Read on!

Trick-or-Treat for UNICEF: Kids Helping Kids

Help the kids who need more than candy and join the fight for the survival of children from MNT. UNICEF gives free fundraising kits with collection boxes each year, but gear up for Halloween 2020 with UNICEF Updates on how to set up a successful fundraising campaign! All funds raised for UNICEF will be used to provide mothers and babies tetanus immunity vaccines, safe materials for childbirth, and resources for sustenance. Don’t forget -- students, parents, businesses, teachers, and so many more people can donate!

Put the "Fun" in Fundraising!

You can:

  • Host events (bakeoffs, color runs, most $)
  • Shout “Trick-or-Treat for UNICEF” for donations instead of candy
  • Sell candy for a dollar at school, such as during lunch periods
  • Host competitions with classmates, friends, and family
  • Collect spare change (lunch money, Wawa runs, etc.)
  • Incentivize! Join celebrities like Selena Gomez, Zendaya, J-Lo in the fight for children’s safety

You are the Hero!

When UNICEF has enough funds to provide supplies, medical staff, immunity vaccines, and education, no mother and child will ever have to be worried about the life-threatening disease. Impoverished future mothers and babies will be immune from MNT and have access to resources and help despite their barriers. You can make it happen.
